## Why These Matter

Diátaxis distinguishes between:

- **How-to guides** — tell you what to do (task-oriented)
- **Explanations** — tell you why something works that way (understanding-oriented)

Explanations help you make good decisions when the documented how-to doesn't quite fit your situation. Understanding the *why* lets you adapt the system intelligently rather than following steps blindly.
